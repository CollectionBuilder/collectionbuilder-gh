Creating Your Collection
For step-by-step instructions on how to build your digital collection, see our:

CollectionBuilder-GH Walkthrough

For those who can't wait, below are six quick steps to get you started:

  1. Create a new project by clicking the green "Use This Template" button on the CollectionBuilder GitHub repository.
  2. Make a copy of the Metadata Template.
  3. Follow the formatting of the example record in the template to fill in metadata about your own objects.
  4. Upload your files into the "objects" folder in your repository.
  5. Activate on GitHub Pages for the Repository.
  6. Check out your site via the link provided on your repository's setting's page!

Find complete details in the CollectionBuilder docs.
